Classroom Sections In PowerSchool, the SIS, teachers are assigned sections. Students are enrolled in those sections in order to access the course content and activities.
Grad Plan Plan of Study and Course Plan
(Naviance®)		 The Plan of Study is a tool that helps students map out their academic coursework to align with their career interests, graduation requirements, and postsecondary goals. Once a Plan of Study is completed, then it becomes the student’s Course Plan.
A Course Plan is their multiyear plan of coursework, equivalent to a four-year plan. With the help of Naviance, it allows students, counselors, and educators to track progress, explore pathways, and ensure students are on the right track toward their future plans.
